About us

The restaurant

The restaurant is created to give you relax while you are eating your healthy dishes

You can sit outside and inside... in every place you are surrounded by the green of the plants, the parfum of the nature.

It's the best place if you want to take a break of the rush of you day, you cna sit reading a book, taking a coffee with you bestfriend, eat the best avocado toast of the city ;-)

The chef

Marco is our chef, he is always researching the best ingredients for the creation of the dishes

He tries everyday to create the best menu for our customers, we want they feel at home and he does the possible to give to our food the magic of an home lunch or dinner.
